About

Hello, I’m Eleonora Tani

When asked what I wanted to be when I was older, my response was always the same:

“I want to be a ballerina!!!”

As a child I was always dressed as Barbie (including a tutu) and had my own doll with me. The only issue with my career plan was that instead of working on my Plie, I was building huge houses out of Lego.

My houses looked weird, everyone said.

Luckily, my career as a dancer never started. Instead, I had the incredible fortune of studying architecture in Florence, which is more an open-air museum than a city. My teachers were some of the best I could choose.

I embraced this profession with the greatest possible passion, loving every single moment of it.

Moving to London I was able to live what is still a daydream: my career has taken off and the passion never fades.

I love to meet clients for the first time, understanding their desires and satisfying them with big projects and small details.

I love spending time on the construction site, seeing my imagination transform into reality.

I am demanding, often overly hard on myself, but that drive allows me to go to sleep satisfied with myself. I am the person I wanted to be, and that says it all.
